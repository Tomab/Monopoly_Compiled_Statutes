MCS 40 BANKER
	Sec. 40-10 Selecting the Banker
	(a) The Banker may be any human entity chosen by mutual agreement by all Players.
	Sec. 40-20 Removing the Banker
	(a) If at any time during the Monopoly Game the Banker should refuse to or become incapable of performing their assigned duties as Banker, or for any reason at all, a Player may move for a vote of no-confidence in the Banker.
	(b) If the motion is carried and the Banker is not a Player, the Players need a unanimous vote against the Banker to compel the Banker to resign.
	(c) If the motion is carried and the Banker is a Player, then Players need an all-but-one vote against the Banker to compel the Banker to resign.
	Sec. 40-30 Resignation
	(a) The Banker may resign at any time and for any reason.
	(b) When the Banker resigns, the Monopoly Game cannot continue until a new Banker is selected by mutual agreement of the Players.
	Sec. 40-40 Personal Funds
	(a) If the Banker is a Player they must keep their personal funds separate from those of the Bank.
