MCS 40 BANKER
	Sec. 40-10 Selecting a Banker
	(a) The Banker may be any human entity chosen by mutual agreement by all Players.
	Sec. 40-20 Re-selecting a Banker
	(a) If at any time during the Monopoly Game the Banker should refuse to or become incapable of performing their assigned duties as banker, or for any reason at all, a Player may move for a vote of no-confidence in the banker.
	(b) If the motion is carried, and the Players unanimously vote against the Banker, the Banker is compelled to resign.
	(c) When a Banker resigns, the game cannot continue until a new Banker is selected by mutual agreement of the Players.
	Sec. 40-30 Personal Funds
	(a) If the selected Banker is also a Player they must keep their personal funds separate from those of the Bank.
