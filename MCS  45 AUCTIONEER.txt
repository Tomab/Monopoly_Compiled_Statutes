MCS 45 AUCTIONEER
	Sec. 45-10 Selecting the Auctioneer
	(a) The Auctioneer may be any human entity chosen by mutual agreement by all Players.
	Sec. 45-20 Removing the Auctioneer
	(a) If at any time during the Monopoly Game the Auctioneer should refuse to or become incapable of performing their assigned duties as Auctioneer, or for any reason at all, a Player may move for a vote of no-confidence in the Auctioneer.
	(b) If the motion is carried and the Auctioneer is not a Player, the Players need a unanimous vote against the Auctioneer to compel the Auctioneer to resign.
	(c) If the motion is carried and the Auctioneer is a Player, then Players need an all-but-one vote against the Auctioneer to compel the Auctioneer to resign.
	Sec. 45-30 Resignation
	(a) The Auctioneer may resign at any time and for any reason.
	(b) When the Auctioneer resigns, the Monopoly Game cannot continue until a new Auctioneer is selected by mutual agreement of the Players.
