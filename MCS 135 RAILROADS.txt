MCS 135 RAILROADS
	Sec. 135-10 Landing on Railroads
	(a) Whenever a Player lands on a Player owned railroad, the Player may choose to move their Token to any other railroad owned by the same Player.
	(b) The Player must pay rent even if they do not choose to travel.
	(c) A Player may travel on their own railroads for free.
	Sec. 135-20 Travel Specifics
	(a) Travel is direct and across the board.
	(b) A Player does not collect $200 in Monopoly Money for passing GO when they travel the railroad.
	(c) Travel must occur on the same turn on which the Player has landed on a railroad.
	Sec. 135-30 Travel Restrictions
	(a) A Player may not travel on Bank-owned railroads.
	(b) A Railroad cannot be used on the same turn that the Property is acquired by any Player.
