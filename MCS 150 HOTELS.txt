MCS 150 HOTELS
	Sec. 150-10 When a Player has four houses on each property of a complete color-group, they may buy a hotel from the Bank and erect it on any property of the color-group.
	(a) The Player returns the four houses from that property to the Bank and pays the price for the hotel as shown on the Title Deed card.
	(b) Only one hotel may be erected on any one property.
