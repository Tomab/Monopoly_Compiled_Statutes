MCS 180 MORTGAGES
	Sec. 180-10 Unimproved Properties can be mortgaged through the Bank at any time.
	(a) Before an Improved Property can be mortgaged, all the Improvements on all the properties of its color-group must be sold back to the Bank.
	(b) The mortgage value is printed on each Title Deed Card.
	(c) No rent can be collected on mortgaged Properties or utilities, but rent can be collected on unmortgaged properties in the same group.
	(d) In order to lift the mortgage, the Owner must pay the Bank the amount of the mortgage plus 10% interest.
	(e) When all the properties of a color-group are no longer mortgaged, the Owner may begin to buy back Improvements at full price.
	(f) The Player who mortgages Property retains possession of it and no other Player may secure it by lifting the mortgage from the Bank.
	(g) The Owner may sell or trade this mortgaged Property to another Player at any agreed price.
