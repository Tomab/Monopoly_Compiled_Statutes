MCS 180 MORTGAGES
	Sec. 180-10 Mortgage Time
	(a) Unimproved Properties can be mortgaged through the Bank at any time.
	(b) Improved Property shall not be mortgaged unless all Improvements on all the properties of its color-group are first sold to the Bank.
	Sec. 180-20 Mortgage Value
	(a) When a Player mortgages a property the Bank shall pay the Player the amount indicated on the back of the Title Deed Card in Monopoly Money.
	(b) When a Property is mortgaged, the Player will place the Title Deed Card face down.
	Sec. 180-30 Rent Collection
	(a) Rent shall not be collected on mortgaged Properties
	(b) Rent can be collected on unmortgaged Properties in the same color-group.
	Sec. 180-40 Lifting the Mortgage
	(a) In order to lift the mortgage, the Owner must pay the Bank the amount of the mortgage plus 10%.
	(b) The Player who mortgages a Property retains possession of it and no other Player may secure the Property by lifting the mortgage from the Bank.
	Sec. 180-50 Trading Mortgaged Properties
	(a) The Owner may sell or trade mortgaged Property to any Player without having to first unmortgage the Property.
