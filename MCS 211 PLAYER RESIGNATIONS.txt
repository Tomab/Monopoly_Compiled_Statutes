MCS 211 PLAYER RESIGNATIONS
	Sec. 210-10 A Player may at any time choose to resign from the Monopoly Game.
	Sec. 210-20 If the resigning Player does not indicate what they wish to do with their Properties and Monopoly Money:
	(a) The money is returned to the Bank.
	(b) Properties are immediately auctioned off by the Auctioneer starting from the lowest priced property.
(c)	Finally, if the resigning Player is in possession of any get out of jail free cards, they will be auctioned off as well.
