MCS 30 PREPARATION
	Sec. 30-10 Place the game board on a table or other suitably flat area.
	Sec. 30-15 Shuffle and place the Chance and Community Chest cards face down on their allotted spaces on the game board.
	Sec. 30-20 An Arbitrator must be chosen.
	Sec. 30-30 A Banker must be chosen.
	Sec. 30-31 The Banker places the free parking reward on the Game Board.
	Sec. 30-40 An Auctioneer must be chosen.
	Sec. 30-50 Each Player must select a Token.
	Sec. 30-60 The Banker must distribute $1,500 to each Player.
