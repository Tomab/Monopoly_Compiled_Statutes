MCS 45 AUCTIONEER
	Sec. 45-10 Selecting a Auctioneer
	(a) The Auctioneer may be any human entity chosen by mutual agreement by all Players.
	Sec. 45-20 Re-selecting a Auctioneer
	(a) If at any time during the Monopoly Game the Auctioneer should refuse to or become incapable of performing their assigned duties as Auctioneer, or for any reason at all, a Player may move for a vote of no-confidence in the Auctioneer.
	(b) If the motion is carried, and the Players unanimously vote against the Auctioneer, the Auctioneer is compelled to resign.
	(c) When an Auctioneer resigns, the game cannot continue until a new Auctioneer is selected by mutual agreement of the Players.
