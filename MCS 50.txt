MCS 50 THE BANK
	Sec. 50-5 Bank Management
	(a) The Bank is solely managed by the Banker.
	(b) The Bank may at any time impose fines and fees for any service.
	(c) The Bank shall set an amount of money to be placed on the Game Board as a Free Parking reward and keep this amount constant for the duration of the Monopoly Game.
	Sec. 50-10 Bank Holdings
	(a) The Bank holds all Monopoly Money not held by the Players, bank-owned Title Deed cards, non-built houses, and non-built hotels.
	Sec. 50-20 Payouts
	(a) The Bank pays salaries, “Chance” card rewards, and “Community Chest” card rewards.
	(b) When paid for, the Bank distributes Title Deed cards to Players.
	(c) When bought, the Bank is responsibly for distributing houses and hotels.
	(d) The Bank disburses Monopoly Money when properties are mortgaged.
	(e) The Bank distributes Monopoly Money when buying houses and hotels back.
	Sec. 50-30 Collections
	(a) The Bank receives Monopoly Money for properties it sells, proceeds from public auctions held by the Auctioneer, houses the Bank has sold, hotels the Bank has sold, and for mortgaged properties.
	Sec. 50-30 Currency
	(a) The Bank defines the accepted forms of currency.
	(b) The Bank may use paper, coin, or any object to represent any denomination the Bank sees fit.
	(c) The Bank may at any time change, add, or remove any type of currency from circulation and at any time demand that any Player exchange any currency they have in their possession.
	(d) The Bank may never run out of money.
	Sec. 50-40 Additional Duties
	(a) The Bank shall be required to make change to any Player upon the Player's reasonable request.
